openblock

just learn for the sake of it

open

Anyone should be able to run a block anywhere in the world and access toolkit that would enable them to do so with ease in the highest possible quality. Imagine you can just pick a block somewhere in the world and go and learn when you can.

non-hierarchical

This is about your learning. While there might be people who prepare the content and guide a group through a block, we are in this together as a community. We can learn from one another. Learn in your own pace and according to your own interests.

block-based

Blocks enable us to focus and get together for a definite period of time. There can be blocks ranging from days to months. This allows you to combine blocks in your own way to design a unique learning path.

nonprofit

We can put our own funds together to fund our own education. People's work on preparing and guiding a block should be fairly compensated, but this should not be a way for someone to profit from people's inherent desire to learn.

openblock should offer:

  • blocks: a database of blocks happening at different locations around the world
  • locations: a database of physical locations that are suitable for running a block
  • people: an online community of people who are interested in attending and running blocks
