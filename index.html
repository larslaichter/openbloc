Welcome to OpenBlock

A toolkit of educational practices that can be picked up by anyone and used to get together a community to run a learning block anywhere in the world.

Non-Hierarchical

While there might be people who prepare the content and guide a group through a block, it is mainly relying on each and every one of the participants to learn according to their own interests and at their own pace. We are all in it together.

Block-Based

Because learning requires focus

Blocks can happen for a specific period of time ranging from just a couple of days to a month when you can dive deep into the topic at hand. This also allows people from different locations to get together in one place. (it would be great to have a team that can help with visas)

Nonprofit

This is not for someone to profit from people's inherent desire to learn. While people's work on preparing a block should be fairly compensated, this should be crowdfunded by the community that will attend. (it would be great to have a fund with scholarships)

OpenBlock Components

  • Blocks: a database of blocks happening at different locations around the world
  • Locations: a database of physical locations that are fit for running a block
  • People: an online community of people who are interested in attending and running blocks
