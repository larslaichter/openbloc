openblock

just learn for the sake of it

open

A toolkit of educational practices that can be picked up by anyone and used to get together a community to run a learning block anywhere in the world.

non-hierarchical

While there might be people who prepare the content and guide a group through a block, it is mainly relying on each and every one of the participants to learn according to their own interests and at their own pace. We are all in it together.

block-based

Because learning requires focus, blocks can happen for a specific period of time ranging from just a couple of days to a month when you can dive deep into the topic at hand. This also allows people from different locations to get together in one place.

nonprofit

This is not for someone to profit from people's inherent desire to learn. While people's work on preparing a block should be fairly compensated, this should be crowdfunded by the community that will attend.

openblock components

  • blocks: a database of blocks happening at different locations around the world
  • locations: a database of physical locations that are suitable for running a block
  • people: an online community of people who are interested in attending and running blocks
