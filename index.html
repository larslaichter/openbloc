openblock

just learn for the sake of it

open

Anyone should be able to run a block and access toolkit that would enable them to do so with ease. Let's have blocks running all around the world.

non-hierarchical

This is about your learning. While there might be people who prepare the content and guide a group through a block, we are in this together as a community. We can learn from one another. Learn in your own pace and according to your own interests.

block-based

Blocks enable us to focus and get together for a definite period of time. There can be blocks ranging from days to months. This allows you to combine to design your unique learning path.

nonprofit

We can put our own funds together to get own education. People's work on preparing and guiding a block should be fairly compensated, but this should not be a way for someone to profit from people's inherent desire to learn.

openblock should offer:

  • blocks: a database of blocks happening at different locations around the world
  • locations: a database of physical locations that are suitable for running a block
  • people: an online community of people who are interested in attending and running blocks
